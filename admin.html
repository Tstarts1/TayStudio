How to Add Painting Images
A quick guide
Option 1 — Imgur (easiest): Upload to imgur.com (free). Right-click the image → Copy Image Address.
Option 2 — Instagram: Open a post, right-click image → Copy Image Address.
Option 3 — Any host: Squarespace, Cloudinary, Google Photos (with public share link).

The URL should end in .jpg, .png, or .webp — it must point directly to the image file.
